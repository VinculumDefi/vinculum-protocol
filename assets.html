This is a representative selection. The full registry contains 1001 approved assets across many chains.

When you commit an asset, it is locked directly on its native chain by the protocol’s spoke vault contract. Your asset never leaves its home chain. Once the lock is recorded locally, the vault sends a single cross-chain message via Axelar to request minting of the corresponding $VCLM on Base. Axelar is used only as a secure messaging layer for the mint instruction — it plays no role in the locking or custody of your committed asset.
