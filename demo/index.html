Before You Begin

Four things every participant should know first.

⛓
This runs on a real blockchain. The demonstration lives on Base mainnet, a public network. Every action you take is a real, verifiable transaction.
⛽
Transactions need gas. Each action costs a small network fee paid in ETH on Base — typically a few cents.
◈
You need a little Base ETH. Before starting, move a small amount of ETH to Base — most exchanges let you withdraw directly to the Base network, or you can bridge from Ethereum.
⓪
The demo tokens are free — and valueless. dASSET, dVCLM, dCHONX, and dSYNTH exist to teach. They are not investments, cannot be sold, and carry no monetary value.
