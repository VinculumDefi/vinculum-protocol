The mechanism

How It
Works

The protocol is simple by design. Lock. Earn. Wait. Repeat. No complexity hidden in the fine print. The rules are the same for everyone.

The longer you commit, the more you earn. Duration is the multiplier. Time is the only advantage you can build.

01
Choose your lock
7-day trial (2.5% fee) or 30+ days (5% fee). Longer duration earns proportionally more VCLM.
02

101+ assets across every major chain. Bitcoin, Ethereum, XRP, LUNC, DGB, SHIB, DOGE, and 94 more.

When your lock expires, your original asset — minus only the disclosed fee — returns to your wallet.

Section 03

The Three
Tokens

Three tokens. Each one necessary for the existence of the next. Each one more scarce than the last. The system only moves in one direction.

VCLM
The Foundation · Hard cap: 10 billion

The primary token. Earned exclusively through vault locks — never purchased. Emission decays 1.667% per month from day one. Every VCLM in existence will have been minted through a commitment. When CHONX launches, VCLM stakers earn 1.5× more CHONX than any other participant.

CHONX
The Middle Layer · Hard cap: 100 billion

Launches automatically when VCLM supply crosses 10,000,000 tokens — a contract execution, not a team decision. A high-volume participation token that broadens the coalition. One of two ingredients required to mint SYNTH.

SYNTH
The Apex · Hard cap: 10 million

The scarcest token in the system. Minted only by permanently burning 1,000 VCLM + 10,000 CHONX. Every SYNTH is the irreversible record of everything destroyed to make it. Permanently deflationary by design. Cannot be created any other way. Ever.

Section 05

Governance That
Actually Matters

Stake VCLM or CHONX to earn a governance seat. SYNTH holders receive seats automatically. Real responsibility. Real power. Real treasury distributions.

Governance sets a budget — for example, 60% Rewards, 30% Exchange Listings, 10% Marketing. Once set, the budget runs automatically every 10-day epoch across the Core Reserve. Governance can change the budget at any time, but is not required to vote every epoch.

Tier 3 · Entry
VCLM

Minimum 1,000 VCLM. Voting weight 1.0×. 50% of treasury distributions. 100 starting seats expanding 10% per epoch when filled.

Tier 2
CHONX

Minimum 5,000 CHONX. Voting weight 2.5×. 30% of treasury distributions. 50 starting seats inheriting Tier 3 expansion cycles.

Tier 1 · Apex
SYNTH

Minimum 1 SYNTH. Voting weight 7.0×. 20% of treasury distributions. Built only through sustained commitment and permanent sacrifice.

Governance lock mechanics: Minimum 10-day lock to hold a seat and vote. Extend using any standard duration interval up to 36 months. The 5% fee applies once at initial entry. Maintaining your seat costs nothing as long as you extend before your lock expires. Auto-extend available for a defined period. If your lock expires without extension, the seat opens for another participant.
