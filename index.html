The Mechanism

Simple by design.

Three steps. No hidden mechanics. No yield farming risk. Your tokens do exactly one thing while they wait — nothing.

STEP 01
🔒
Lock your tokens

Choose any approved asset, an amount, and a duration — 7 days to 730 days. Approve the contract. Commit. Your tokens transfer to the VaultManager contract and stay there. No lending. No farming. No risk from other protocols. They just wait.

→
STEP 02
⬡
Earn $VCLM instantly

The moment you commit, VCLM is minted to your wallet. Calculated from deposit value, asset tier, and duration multiplier. Longer commitment = more VCLM. There is no other way to acquire it.

→
STEP 03
✓
Withdraw at maturity

At maturity, withdraw your principal. 5% is permanently retained by the protocol treasury — governed by VCLM holders. Your VCLM was already in your wallet from day one. You don't wait for the reward. You already have it.

"Vinculum is not a Base protocol that bridges to other chains. It is a multi-chain protocol that calls Base home."

Every VaultManager on every chain connects to the same VCLM token contract on Base via Axelar Interchain Token Service. VCLM minted on BNB Chain is the same token as VCLM minted on Ethereum — same supply, same governance weight, same value.

This is what Axelar was built for. Vinculum uses it the way it was intended — not as a bridge, but as the foundation of a genuinely interchain protocol.
