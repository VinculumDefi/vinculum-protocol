How It Works

Three steps. No tricks.

Vinculum is a commitment vault protocol. You lock assets, the protocol mints VCLM proportional to your commitment, and your principal comes back at maturity.

01
Lock Your Assets

Choose any supported token and a commitment duration — from 7 days (trial) up to 730 days. Your assets are held in a VaultManager smart contract on their native chain. They never bridge. They never move.

Non-custodial
02
Earn $VCLM

The protocol mints VCLM proportional to your commitment value, duration, and asset tier. 10 VCLM per dollar at launch — decaying 20% per year. Early commitment earns the most, forever.

Proof of commitment
03
Reclaim Principal

At maturity, 95% of your committed value returns. The remaining 5% enters the protocol treasury permanently — split 82/12/6 across Core Reserve, Dev Fund, and Ecosystem Growth. No one receives it.

95% returned
The Trial Vault

Try it before you commit.

The 7-day trial vault lets you experience the protocol risk-free. At day 7, you decide. Continue into a standard vault — or exit with 97.5% of your principal returned. No surprise fees. No lock-in.

If you continue: the remaining 2.5% joins the initial 2.5% in the treasury. If you exit: only 2.5% is retained. Either way, VCLM is prorated to your actual time committed.

Governance Council

The protocol doesn't run itself. The Council runs the protocol.

Vinculum does not have validators in the traditional sense. Base handles the chain. What we have instead are Governance Council members — Trustees, Stewards, and Council Members — whose responsibility is to ensure this protocol never goes quiet, never drifts, and is always ready to react, propose, and improve. The chain keeps the contracts alive. The Council keeps the protocol alive.

Protocol Treasury

Every vault feeds the protocol.
Nobody feeds themselves.

The 5% entry fee from every vault commitment splits atomically at the smart contract level. No individual receives treasury funds. Deployment of treasury assets is decided by governance vote of VCLM holders.

82%
Core Reserve

The long-term security fund of the protocol. Held in the treasury and governed by the Governance Council. Used for audits, protocol security, and long-term stability.

12%
Dev Fund

Sustained development of the protocol. Controlled by the founding team during the pre-transition period. Transfers to governance control after the three milestone transition is complete.

6%
Ecosystem Growth

Exchange listings, liquidity pools, partnerships, and community programs. Deployed by governance vote. Every use of these funds requires a successful Steward-tier proposal.
