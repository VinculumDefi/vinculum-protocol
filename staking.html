The Fault Mechanism

When an anomaly is detected — oracle failure, bridge exploit, or contract alert — new deposits can be paused by governance. Withdrawals can never be paused under any circumstance. Your principal is always accessible, always withdrawable, no matter what.

🚫
Deposits Paused
When a fault is active, no new vault commitments can be created. Disabled at the smart contract level — not just the frontend.
✅
Withdrawals Always Open
Exit functions are immutable and always accessible. No governance vote or emergency power can ever block a withdrawal.
🗳️
Governance Resolves
Only a governance vote by qualified $VCLM holders can lift a pause. Every action is on-chain and fully auditable.
$VCLM Token

The protocol's only token.
Earned. Never bought.

🔒
No Presale. No Premine.
Every VCLM ever minted was earned through commitment. Not the founders. Not the team. Not early investors. 100% community-earned — permanently and immutably.
🗳️
Governance Rights
VCLM holders vote on protocol parameters, new vault assets, and treasury deployment. Three tiers — Trustees, Stewards, and Council Members — with increasing authority.
🌐
Interchain Native
Built on Axelar ITS from day one. One unified VCLM supply across all chains — not fragmented bridged tokens. Committed assets never leave their native chain.
📉
Emission Decay
Emission decreases 20% per year, floored at 10% of original rate. The longer you wait to start, the less you earn when you do.
