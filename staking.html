⚠ Fault Mechanism
The Vinculum Protocol includes a guardian fault detection system. When an anomaly is detected — oracle failure, bridge exploit, or contract alert — new deposits can be paused by governance. Withdrawals can never be paused under any circumstance. Your principal is always accessible.
